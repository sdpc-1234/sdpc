Vision

To impart superlative technical education for the rural students to accomplish their goals as a scholar, industry ready employee and an innovative entrepreneur thereby serving the society.

.

Mission

To provide best-in-class infrastructure, and innovative teaching techniques to foster professional competence. To empower the students by nurturing their creativity, innovation, technical ability and entrepreneurship skills to face the societal challenges. To collaborate with industries and other organizations for transforming the students to job ready professionals and life-long learners. To inculcate ethical values and discipline among the students for the betterment of society.
