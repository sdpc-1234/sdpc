About College-Journey of the Institution

  • Lakshmikanthammal Educational Trust, a Telugu Public Educational Trust established in 1994, rendering philanthropic and educational service to Telugu linguistic people as well as others, started an engineering college in the name and style of R. M. K. Engineering College in 1995-96 at Kavaraipettai, Gummidipoondi Taluk, Thiruvallur District. The same Trust has also started a Polytechnic College in the name and style of SRI DURGADEVI POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE to impart technical education to the economically weaker sections of the society.
  • SRI DURGADEVI POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE started in the year 1998-99, was inaugurated on 2nd November 1998. The Polytechnic College is situated at R. S. M. Nagar, Kavaraipettai, adjacent to R. M. K. Engineering College. Sri Durgadevi Polytechnic College is approved by AICTE and affiliated to the Directorate of Technical Educaiton.
    • Our college is a Telugu Linguistic minority, self-financing institution. Our college is located at Kavaraipettai, at a distance of about 35 K.M from Chennai on the Chennai – Vijayawada National Highways.
  • The college is started to impart technical education to the economically weaker section of the society.
  • The college is also accessible by suburban trains from Chennai.

About Vision & Mission

Vision
  • To impart superlative technical education for the rural students to accomplish their goals as a scholar, industry ready employee and an innovative entrepreneur thereby serving the society.
Mission
  • To provide best-in-class infrastructure, and innovative teaching techniques to foster professional competence.
  • To empower the students by nurturing their creativity, innovation, technical ability and entrepreneurship skills to face the societal challenges.
  • To collaborate with industries and other organizations for transforming the students to job ready professionals and life-long learners.
  • To inculcate ethical values and discipline among the students for the betterment of society.
